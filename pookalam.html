About

Onam is a vibrant festival celebrated in Kerala, India, marking the homecoming of the legendary King Mahabali. Pookalam, an integral part of Onam, is a beautiful floral arrangement created with various colorful flowers, symbolizing prosperity and joy. This tradition showcases the rich cultural heritage and artistic skills of the people of Kerala.
